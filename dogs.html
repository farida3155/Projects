Adoption Form

Welcome to Petco Animals Adoption Form

Thank you for your interest in adopting! Please complete this questionnaire to help assess if your household is ready to responsibly care for a pet.

Adoption Service Fee

If accepted, a non-refundable fee of 600 EGP applies. This includes:

  • 60-minute online interview
  • Life-long post-adoption support
  • One free consultation with rehoming expert Amina El Shazly
  • Discounts from our partners
  • Scientific-based adoption guidance resources

Important Notes

  • Adoption is a serious, long-term commitment — household agreement is essential.
  • Submitting the form does not guarantee adoption.
  • Minimum age to adopt: 21 years.
  • You can review/save your application at the bottom of the form.
  • If approved, a Zoom interview will be scheduled.
  • If not contacted within one week, the application was not approved.
